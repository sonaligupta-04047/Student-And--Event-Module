Campus-Facilities

Hall of Residence

The Halls of Residence at Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR), formerly known as CET Bhubaneswar, serve as a vibrant second home for students across its expansive campus. The university offers several specialized living spaces, including the Ramanujan Hall of Residence (RHR) and APJ Abdul Kalam Hall of Residence (APJAHR) for boys, along with the Kasturba Hall of Residence (KHR) and Kalpana Chawla Hall of Residence (KCHR) for girls. Together, these hostels provide safe and comfortable accommodation for over 1,100 students. A major highlight of staying at OUTR is the organized mess facility, which provides three nutritious meals daily, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Students enjoy facilities like Wi-Fi connectivity, sports areas, study environments, and secure accommodation with dedicated wardens.

Central Library

The Central Library at Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR) is an important academic resource center supporting learning and research. It contains more than 41,000 books and thousands of digital journals and e-books from publishers like IEEE and ScienceDirect. The library also provides reading rooms, online research facilities, semester book bank services, and a peaceful study atmosphere for students.

Sports and Fitness

OUTR provides excellent sports and fitness facilities for students. The campus includes playgrounds for cricket, football, hockey, basketball, volleyball, badminton, and indoor games. Students can also access a gymnasium and participate in annual athletic meets and inter-college tournaments, encouraging physical fitness and teamwork.

Canteens

The canteen facilities at OUTR serve as important dining and social spaces for students and faculty. The central canteen provides snacks, tea, and meals at affordable prices, while hostel mess facilities provide breakfast, lunch, and dinner for residential students. The university continuously works toward improving hygiene, maintenance, and food quality.

Clubs and Technical Societies

OUTR hosts various technical, cultural, and social clubs that enhance student life beyond academics. Technical societies like Zairza, SAE OUTR, and the Energy Club help students develop practical skills through projects, hackathons, and workshops. Cultural clubs organize music, dance, drama, and social service activities, helping students improve leadership, teamwork, creativity, and communication skills.
